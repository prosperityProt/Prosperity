How it's work ?

Tax

Prosperity adopts the contemporary Token 2022 standard on the Solana blockchain, incorporating a 10% transaction tax. This tax is allocated with 2.5% directed to the Operations wallet and 7.5% distributed to token holders in the form of SOL rewards.

Hold To Earn $SOL

Taxes gathered from transactions are automatically converted into Solana and shared among all eligible holders, excluding the deployer and liquidity pool accounts. To qualify for distributions, you must hold a minimum of 0.01 Solana.

Auto Rewards

Sol are auto rewarded on your wallet. Rewards are optimized by advanced mathematical models to ensure they are maximized in an efficient and automated manner.
